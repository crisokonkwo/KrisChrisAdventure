FAQ

Will the ceremony be outside?

No! Although if weather permits, there is a chance we might be outdoors.

Are children allowed at the ceremony?

Yes, we are excited to have the little critters celebate with us!

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

A couple of snaps are allowed, just don't experience the whole event through your camera.

Will there be parking available?

There will be plenty of parking available no valet though. Please plan to arrive early if you need to park.

Will there be vegetarian options?

Yes! We will have a variety of options to meet some people dietary needs.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes! Transportation will be provided from the two hotel blocks to OceanCliff and back after the event.

Dress Code Tips for Our Special Days

Rehearsal/Cocktail Night

Dress Code: Casual

This is a relaxed and fun evening, so keep it comfortable yet stylish!

  • Ladies: Flowy dresses, jumpsuits, or chic separates – something casual but fashionable.
  • Gentlemen: Well-fitted shirts, chinos, or casual blazers. Keep it easygoing and polished.
Wedding Day

Dress Code: Cocktail/Semi-Formal

Celebrate with us in elegant, semi-formal attire that’s perfect for a memorable occasion.

  • Ladies: Cocktail dresses, midi dresses, or any other semi-formal outfit that makes you feel fabulous.
  • Gentlemen: Suits or blazers are great, but no need to go overly formal – just look sharp and stylish.

We can’t wait to celebrate with you and see your amazing looks!
